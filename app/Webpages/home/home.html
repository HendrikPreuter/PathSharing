What is role-playing?

Role-playing is a fun and social activity where you come together in a group of usually four to fife players. One of these player takes on the role as game master (also known as: dungeon master or story-teller), this player will create a world for the other players to have adventures. The gm sets the scenes by describing what the players see, smell hear and feel. Much like the descriptions that can be found in books, hence the term story-teller. The gm also plays all the non-player-characters(NPCs) the players can interact with.
